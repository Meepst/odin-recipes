Cosmic Brownies

Rich dark chocolate tones balanced by sweet, savory candy coated chocolate sprinkles.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cups brown sugar
  • 5 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 egg and 1 egg yolk, room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract

For The Ganache

  • 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream, room temperature
  • candy coated chocolate sprinkles

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350*F and line a 8 x 8 pan with parchment paper, set aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl sift together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt, set aside.
  3. In a seperate bowl whisk together the melted butter and brown sugar until homogenous. Add eggs, honey, and vanilla extract and whisk until combined, about 1 minute.
  4. Stir in the dry ingredients until no clumps of flour or cocoa remain. Pour the batter into your prepared pan and bake for 20 to 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the middle comes out mostly clean. Allow the brownies to cool
  5. While the brownies cool, make your ganache. In a double boiler, melt the chocolate chips, whisking constantly to amke sure they do not burn. Slowly pour in the cream, whisking constantly until smooth and combined. Pour over the cooled brownies and let set for 20 minutes. Sprinkle with chocolate candies and enjoy!
