Turkey Sandwich

Healthy, filling turkey sandwich perfect for the hot summer weather!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 4 ounces sliced or shaved deli turkey
  • 4 slices Provolone cheese (or your favorite sliced cheese)
  • Green leaf lettuce
  • 2 slices fresh tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Whatever else you enjoy for toppings!

Instructions

  1. Lay out all ingredients
  2. Spread mayonnaise on bread slices and layer one slice with sliced turkey, cheese, lettuce, tomato, salt and pepper, and any other additional toppings.
  3. Top sandwich with remaining bread slice, cut into halves, and serve.
